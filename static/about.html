My Story

My name is Frank Hucek. I am a self-taught furniture maker based in Heber City, UT.
I specialize in creating heirloom furniture, my work emphasizes inlay and ornamentation, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern styling.
Each piece is built to last, showcasing the natural beauty of the wood!
